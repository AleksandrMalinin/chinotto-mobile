# Chinotto Mobile - Product Brief

## What it is

Chinotto Mobile is a lightweight companion app for one job: capturing thoughts the moment they appear.

It is intentionally simple. It helps users save ideas fast on phone, then revisit and develop them on desktop.

## The problem it solves

Ideas appear in motion and disappear quickly. Most apps add friction at that moment with folders, tags, or setup choices.

Chinotto removes those decisions so users can record the thought first and organize later.

## Product promise

- Open app, type immediately
- Save in seconds
- Keep capture calm and low-friction
- Make thoughts available across devices with optional sync

## Core experience

- **Capture-first:** brief brand splash, then direct input
- **Fast submit:** add thought, clear input, continue writing
- **Recent recall:** lightweight stream of recent entries
- **Search:** find entries by text
- **Share in (iOS):** send text/links from other apps into Chinotto
- **Optional sync:** Sign in with Apple + Firebase sync that does not block capture

## Product boundaries

Chinotto Mobile is not a notes workspace. It does not aim to be:

- a document editor
- a project manager
- a knowledge-base tool

Deliberately out of scope:

- folders, tags, categories
- complex editing flows
- heavy dashboards or discovery feeds

## Platform focus

- **Current priority:** iOS shipping quality and sync reliability
- **Android:** planned after iOS priorities are complete

## Success criteria

Chinotto Mobile succeeds when it feels like a pocket for thoughts:

- capture is immediate
- friction stays near zero
- users trust that each thought is safely saved
